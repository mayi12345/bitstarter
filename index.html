Why this idea?

Do you know why people who joined a social group are more likely to live longer? According to a book written by Dr. Leonard Mlodinow, the advantage of belonging to a cohesive society is that "the group is often better equipped to deal with threats from the outside" (Next time, do not blame yourself if your mother complains about you being on Facebook for too long). Patients and care givers, are more desperately need a social and educational network combined, to help them find other patients with similar issues, to learn from & support each other. That's why Haalthy is a MUST.

How does it help?

So do you have a VERY SPECIFIC question about your health? e.g. why I tried to run 30 minutes a day and 5 days a week for 2 months but never lose any weight, or how can I find out the drugs my doctors prescribe for me is the best for me. The best bet is, you are very likely find someone using Haalthy with the exact same question. Even better, we have a large database that you (or your care giver) can use to find scientific evidence. Next time, you can bring up a smart question to discuss with your doctor. Remember, your health is carrying with you for life-time, so definitely ask the accurate question :)

FAQ

That sounds like a great idea! But what about my privacy?
We are so glad that you asked! Your privacy is our top priority. You have complete control over all your data and information. By the way, unlike PatientsLikeMe or Facebook, we do not sell your data to anyone or any companies. One exception, however, is that you agree on giving your data to research organizations, which will use your data to improve drug development, or discover personalized medicine for you and the likes.
I cannot wait to use the site! When will you guys be ready for us to use?
Thank you for your support! Our engineers are working really hard to a. seamlessly integrate useful scientific research on our site; b. testing the prototype of the site. We are looking to put our baby for public view at the end of September. Of course, we will send you an email :)
So if you are not selling our data to companies, how can you make money?
Good question! The answer is, we are not sure yet either. We care more about our customers (You!) and people than money. We might run it as non-profit forever, or we will soon provide some services (i.e. in-home service) that are truly helpful to the patients.
